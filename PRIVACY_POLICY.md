**We do not collect any personal or sensitive user data.**

Our App is designed to function entirely offline and does not require, request, or store any user information. Specifically, we do **NOT** collect:

- Personal identification information (name, email address, phone number, etc.)
- Financial or payment information
- Authentication credentials (usernames, passwords)
- Location data (precise or approximate)
- Contact information or phonebook data
- Device identifiers or unique IDs
- Photos, videos, or media files
- Microphone or camera data
- Health or fitness data
- Browsing history or search queries
- SMS, call log, or messaging data
- Any other personally identifiable information (PII)

Since we do not collect any user data, we do not use, process, or analyze any personal information. The App operates independently on your device without transmitting data to external servers.

## Data Storage and Security

Since our App does not collect any personal data, there is no personal information stored on our servers or transmitted over the internet. All game progress and settings are stored locally on your device and remain under your control.
